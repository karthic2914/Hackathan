THE INTERNATIONAL HACKATHON 2017 DEMO DAY FINALISTS

NOVEMBER 3, 2017

During 4 months, 28 startups in 10 countries participated in a co-working phase. They worked together with BNP Paribas experts in business, marketing, IT, UX, legal, compliance, among others, to validate their solutions and build an MVP who answers a particular challenge. Thanks to the outstanding results during the co-working and the interest of BNP Paribas entities to continue working with them, 16 of those startups were selected as Demo Day Finalists. They received a cash prize or incubation equivalent and the invitation to participate in the BNP Paribas International Hackathon Demo Day that will take place in Paris on the 1st December 2017. In the Demo Day, they will have 3 minutes to convince a jury composed by the BNP Paribas Top Management about their solutions. 3 award categories have been selected in line with the BNP Paribas 2020 Transformation plan. There will be one startup awarded per category, receiving an additional cash prize: “Customer / Employee experience benefit”: The winning solution in this category helps BNP Paribas to improve significantly the experience of either: Our customers during a specific journey with the bank or a part of it, OR Our employees while delivering advices & services to our customers. Digital experience would be the most appreciated.

“Business Potential”:

The winning solution in this category helps BNP Paribas to either: Create a new business opportunity, OR To increase significantly an existing revenue stream. The shortest term the benefit can be seen is the best. “International integration & scalability”: The winning solution in this category is simple to adapt, scale and integrate into BNP Paribas production environment at an international level.
